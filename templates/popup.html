SR Blogs Features

  • Free access to view the blog for all users.
  • Users can add categories.
  • Users can add, edit, and delete the posts they have created.
  • Users can view comments posted by the public on their posts.
  • Unlimited lifetime access to your posts and comments.
  • To create , edit posts & add comments you must sign in.
  • Only registered users can manage their own posts.
  • Users cannot delete categories once added.
  • Unregistered users cannot add or edit posts.
  • Users cannot edit other's posts.

Enjoy exploring our blog and sharing your thoughts!
